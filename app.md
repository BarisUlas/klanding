> Unlike apps that rely on slow, third-party encoders, Kompresso uses Apple's native libraries for blazing-fast, high-efficiency video compression. That means:

> • Minimal battery and storage usage
>
> It's built to work seamlessly on iOS and iPadOS, offering a smooth, responsive, and privacy-focused user experience.

> - **Codec Options** — Use H.264 for wide compatibility or H.265 (HEVC) to shrink video sizes even further.
> - **Advanced Settings** — Fine-tune frame rate, bitrate, and resolution to strike the perfect balance between quality and compression.

## Key Features (extracted)

- Hardware-accelerated compression using Apple's native encoders (fast, low battery)
- **Simple presets**: High / Medium / Low quality — one-tap compression
- **Advanced/Pro controls**: bitrate, encoding (H.264 / H.265), resolution (720p/1080p/4K/Original), FPS, format (MP4/MOV/M4V)
- **Batch compression**: select and export multiple videos at once ("Unified Mode")
- Live size + savings preview (e.g. "43 MB from 515 MB · 91% smaller")
